Executive Overview

What is the DD Sports Intelligence Platform?

The DD Sports Management Intelligence Platform is a comprehensive SaaS management system built specifically for elite football agencies. It centralizes every aspect of player representation — from performance analytics and contract management to scouting pipelines and document vaults — into a single secure, role-based command center.

Built to replace fragmented spreadsheets, scattered communications, and disconnected tools, the platform gives agency owners, agents, scouts, and players a tailored digital workspace — each with the right data, the right controls, and the right interface for their role.

Industry Pain Points

The Challenges Agencies Face Today

Modern football agencies operate at elite levels with outdated infrastructure. These are the most critical operational gaps the platform addresses.

Scattered Player Data

Player information spread across WhatsApp groups, personal emails, and disconnected spreadsheets — no single source of truth for the agency.

Critical

No Performance Visibility

Tracking match ratings and form trends across a full roster manually is error-prone, time-consuming, and always out of date.

High Impact

Contract Blind Spots

Expiry dates missed, renewal windows lost. Agencies operate reactively on contracts instead of managing them proactively with advance alerts.

Revenue Risk

Scouting Bottlenecks

Scout reports in PDFs, target notes in personal emails, recruitment stages untracked — no pipeline visibility for agency leadership.

Inefficiency

Access Control Gaps

Players, scouts, and agents accessing sensitive data they should not see. No role-based access framework means confidential information leaks internally.

Security Risk

Document Chaos

Contracts, passports, medical clearances, and FIFA registrations scattered across personal desktops and shared drives — no structure, no audit trail.

Security Architecture

Enterprise-Grade Security

Built from day one with security as a foundation — not an afterthought. Agency data is sensitive. The platform treats it that way.

Role-Based Access Control

Four distinct user roles (Owner, Agent, Scout, Player) with granular, per-module permissions. Users see only what their role allows — nothing more.

Session Management

JWT authentication with automatic session expiry and refresh token rotation. Idle sessions are terminated automatically to prevent unauthorized access.

Audit Logging

Every data access, edit, and download is logged with user identity, timestamp, and action type. Full audit trail for compliance and internal oversight.

Data Encryption

AES-256 encryption at rest for all stored documents and player data. TLS 1.3 enforced for all data in transit. Internal-access-only architecture with no public API endpoints.
